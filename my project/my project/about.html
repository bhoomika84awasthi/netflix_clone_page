About Us

Welcome to Netflix, the world’s leading streaming entertainment service. We provide a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more – all personalized to your taste.

Our Mission

Our mission is to entertain the world. Whatever your taste, and no matter where you live, we give you access to best-in-class TV shows, movies, and documentaries. Our members control what they want to watch, when they want it, with no ads, in one simple subscription.

Our Story

Founded in 1997, Netflix has grown from a DVD rental service to a global leader in streaming entertainment. Our journey began with mailing DVDs and has evolved to bringing incredible stories from all over the world to our members through their screens.

Leadership

We are led by a seasoned team of executives who have extensive experience in the entertainment and technology industries. Our leadership is dedicated to providing the best service possible for our members and partners.
